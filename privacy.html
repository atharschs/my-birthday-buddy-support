Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

My Birthday Buddy is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your personal information should stay personal.

Data Storage

All birthday information you enter into this app is stored:

  • Locally on your device
  • In your personal iCloud account (if enabled)

Your data is never sent to our servers, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose other than providing you with birthday reminders.

What We Collect

The app stores only the information you provide:

  • Friend names
  • Birthday dates
  • Optional photos
  • Optional notes
  • Notification preferences
  • Group assignments

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your birthday data syncs across your Apple devices signed in with the same Apple ID. This sync is managed entirely by Apple's iCloud service and follows Apple's privacy policies.

Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Edit or delete any birthday at any time
  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Disable iCloud sync in your device settings

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not collect any analytics, usage data, or tracking information. We have no idea how you use the app, and we like it that way.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone. All data you enter stays on your device and your personal iCloud account.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at tonysstuffhelp@gmail.com

By using My Birthday Buddy, you agree to this privacy policy.
