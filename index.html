Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a birthday?

Tap the + button in the top right corner, fill in your friend's name and birthday, and optionally add a photo and notes.

How do notifications work?

You can choose to be reminded the day before, on their birthday, or both. Set your preferred reminder time in Settings.

Does my data sync between devices?

Yes! If you're signed into iCloud, your birthdays automatically sync across all your Apple devices.

How do I use the widget?

Long-press your home screen, tap the + button, search for "My Birthday Buddy", and add the widget in your preferred size.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your data is stored only on your device and your personal iCloud account. We never see or share your information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
